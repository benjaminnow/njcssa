Mission

These are the main goals of the New Jersey Computer Science Student Alliance(NJCSSA)

Programming

We teach kids to program, make apps, make games, and make websites.

How to learn

Mastering the skill of learning something on your own is more important than learning a particular programming language. We teach kids to learn independently so that they can keep programming at home.

Giving Back

By volunteering your time, you can give kids in Newark oportunities to succeed in the twenty-first century.
