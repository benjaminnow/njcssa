Long-term Goals



Tech-House

  • A well-equipped Tech space for kids in Newark
  • Conveniently located next to NJIT and Rutgers Newark campuses
  • Flexible operating hours
  • No tuition or fees for students
  • It will set up a pipleine for skilled talent to flow into Newark

Corporate Partnerships

The NJCSSA project will operate out of the Urban League of Essex County's office in Newark until rennovations on the Tech House are completed. Organizations such as Google and Panasonic have expressed interest in this project. Depending on the success of the small scale pilot in the Urban League's office, funds from corporate sponsors may be available.




History



  • 2012

    Newark Kids Code is founded

    The purpose of Newark Kids Code was to give a first class computer science education to talented Newark inner city youth.

  • 2014-2018

    Newark Kids Code runs several coding bootcamps and workshops with the help of NJIT students.

    Need info here

  • 2015

    The Tech House is bought.

    The Urban League of Essex County buys a victorian house in West Newark. They have hopes of it becoming a tech center. First, they will need to find funding for it.

  • September 2018

    NJCSSA is founded.

    NJCSSA is founded to provide skilled instructors to kids in the Newark Kids Code program. Successful results will lead to partnerships with large tech companies and will provide the necessary money to get the Tech House project off the ground.

Current Situation

508 CENTRAL AVENUE NEWARK, NJ 07107

For the first couple of months, we will be working out of a building owned by the Urban League of Essex County. There are about 40 laptops there, and students hungry to learn will be brought in. The only thing we are missing are great teachers/instructors. The purpose of this temporary setup is to demonstrate to our potential corporate sponsors that we can effectively use any grant money they give us.
