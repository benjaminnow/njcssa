Long-term Goals



Tech-House

  • It is a permanent tech space for kids in Newark
  • Conveniently located next to NJIT and Rutgers Newark campuses
  • It will provide a safe space for learning
  • It will set up a pipleine for skilled talent to flow into Newark

Corporate Partnerships

Corporations like Google and Panasonic are currenly interested in this development. If we can prove that NJCSSA works on a small scale at the Urban League, we are likely to get funding. We need funding because renovations to the tech house are going to cost around $200,000
