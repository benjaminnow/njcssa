Volunteering

NJCSSA makes it easy to volunteer

Flexible Time Commitment

Choose your own days and hours you can volunteer. This can be 1 day a week, or it can be 7 days a week.

Expert Coders not Needed

We are not looking for people who are the best programmers. We want people who are passionate about CS and teaching others.

College ECs

This kind of hands-on, high-impact CS volunteer work will look great for college.
