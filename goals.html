Tech House


The Tech House is a project of the Urban League of Essex County and Newark Kids Code, a program of ULEC and whose director is Darrin Sharif. The goal of Tech House is to provide a 24/7 learning space for Newark students in technical areas. The NJCSSA will help to make Tech House a success by providing classes through our instructors and curriculum once it is operating. We will also help to promote fundraising efforts for the Tech House.
