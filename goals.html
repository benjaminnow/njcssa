Tech House


The Tech House is a mutual goal between Newark Kids Code and the New Jersey Computer Science Student Alliance. The Tech House is a project run by Darrin Sharif, whose goal is to provide a 24/7 learning space for Newark students in technical areas. The NJCSSA will help to make Tech House a success by providing classes through our instructors and curriculum once it is up and running. We will also help to promote fundraising efforts for the Tech House.
